Our Process
  1. The initial phase is about working together collaboratively to come up with the best ideas that will drive traffic to your website. The main goal is to understand the current style of your blog and improve upon it.
  2. The second phase is about hardening those ideas and refining them into a more detailed and clear outlines, once we confirm the outlines we can start writing.
  3. We will then send you a draft of the article within the agreed upon timeline.
  4. You can leave any suggestions or comments and we will do the edits.
  5. Once you are fully happy with the content, we can get to the payment
